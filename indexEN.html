Privacy Policy for the "Moja Parafia" Mobile Application

Last updated: February 7, 2026

1. General Provisions

The Data Administrator is Michał Schmude, operating at mivs.dev, email: biuro.mojaparafia@gmail.com (hereinafter referred to as the "Administrator"). The Administrator makes every effort to protect the privacy of the "Moja Parafia" application users.

2. Type and Source of Processed Data

The application processes data in the following categories:

  • Parish and Clergy Data: Parish name, address, office email, phone number, and the parish priest's name. This is publicly available data obtained from official diocese websites of the Catholic Church and parish notice boards.
  • Location (GPS): The application accesses the device's location to display the user's position on the map and provide navigation to the parish. This data is processed "live" and is not stored on the Administrator's servers.
  • User Data: Email address (only when using the "Suggest a change" feature) and anonymous advertising identifiers.
  • Payments and Subscriptions: Information about Premium purchases is processed by Google Play Billing. The Administrator does not have access to payment card details.
  • Priest/Admin Panel: Access tokens and PIN codes used exclusively for authorizing the editing of announcements.

3. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

Data processing is based on:

  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate Interest): Providing informational services (parish data) and ensuring application stability and security.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (Consent): For data voluntarily provided by users (change proposal forms, priest logins).
  • General Decree on the Protection of Personal Data of Individuals in the Church: Regarding the publication of public information about clergy functions.

4. Data Security

We use modern protection measures, including:

  • Encryption of connections using the HTTPS (SSL/TLS) protocol.
  • Two-factor authentication for parish administrators (Token + PIN).
  • Data minimization principles – we only collect what is necessary for the application to function.

5. Data Recipients (Third-Party Services)

To provide full functionality, the application integrates the following services:

  • Google Maps API: Map display and navigation.
  • Google AdMob: Displaying advertisements (in the free version).
  • Google Play Billing: Handling secure payments for the Premium version.
  • Firebase: Stability analysis (Crashlytics) and Push notifications.

6. Rights of Data Subjects

According to GDPR, every user has the right to:

  • Access, rectify, and delete their data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to the processing of location data (via Android system settings).
  • File a complaint with the supervisory authority (President of the Personal Data Protection Office - PUODO).

Contact for privacy matters: biuro.mojaparafia@gmail.com.

7. "Suggest a Change" Feature

Data provided in the change proposal form is used solely to verify the reliability of the information. Providing proposals is completely voluntary.

8. Data Retention Period

  • Location data: 0 seconds (processed only in RAM while the map is active).
  • Email addresses from forms: Until the change is verified (maximum 12 months).
  • Parish data: For the duration of the informational service.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

The Administrator reserves the right to make changes to this document. The current version is always available at mivs.dev and within the application.
